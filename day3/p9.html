iPhone

The iPhone is Apple’s flagship smartphone, known for its innovative technology, sleek design, and powerful performance. Experience cutting-edge features like Face ID, advanced cameras, and unparalleled speed.

  • Display: Super Retina XDR display with Ceramic Shield
  • Processor: A17 Bionic chip for unrivaled performance
  • Camera: 12 MP Ultra-Wide camera, 4K video recording
  • Battery: Up to 20 hours of video playback
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB options
  • Operating System: iOS 17 with advanced features

AirPods

Apple AirPods provide the ultimate wireless audio experience. With crystal-clear sound, noise cancellation, and seamless integration with Apple devices, they are the perfect companion for all your listening needs.

  • Audio Quality: High-fidelity sound with spatial audio
  • Battery Life: Up to 6 hours of listening time
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0 with seamless Apple integration
  • Compatibility: Works with all Apple devices

iPad

The iPad combines portability with incredible performance, making it perfect for work, creativity, and entertainment. With a stunning display and compatibility with the Apple Pencil, it’s designed for productivity and more.

  • Display: 12.9-inch Liquid Retina display with ProMotion
  • Processor: M2 chip for ultimate performance
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB options
  • Compatibility: Supports Apple Pencil (2nd generation)
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours of surfing the web on Wi‑Fi

MacBook

The MacBook is the epitome of power and portability. With cutting-edge processors, brilliant Retina displays, and a sleek design, it is the ultimate tool for professionals and creatives alike.

  • Display: 13-inch Retina display with True Tone
  • Processor: Apple M2 chip for next-level performance
  • Storage: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB SSD options
  • Battery Life: Up to 18 hours of web browsing
  • Ports: Two Thunderbolt/USB 4 ports
